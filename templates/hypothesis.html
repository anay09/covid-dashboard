The aim for the below hypothesis testing was to find if the death of an individual due to corona depends on the age of the individual. The data used for the same is of South Korea . The dataset contained the age of the paitents and their status. We preprocessed the data and divided patients into different age groups of according to their age. Finally we did Chi-Square testing to check the dependency between death due to corona and age of the patient.

Null Hypothesis

Confirmed cases and Number of deaths is independent of age

Alternate Hypothesis

Irrespective of number of confirmed cases, number of deaths increase with increase in age
